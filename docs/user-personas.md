# Persona Comparison

| Persona 1                     | Persona 2                                |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Experienced Software Engineer | Computer Science Student                 |
| Shares articles and resources | Asks questions and learns                |
| Creates News posts            | Creates Ask posts                        |
| Participates in discussions   | Seeks advice from the community          |
| Interested in industry trends | Interested in learning and career growth |

## How They Use the Platform

### Persona 1: Software Engineer

* Shares technology articles and resources.
* Upvotes valuable content.
* Participates in technical discussions.
* Helps answer community questions.

### Persona 2: Computer Science Student

* Creates Ask posts to seek guidance.
* Learns from community discussions.
* Reads articles shared by professionals.
* Seeks career and interview advice.
